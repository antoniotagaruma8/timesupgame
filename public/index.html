Classroom Vocabulary Challenge

How to Play

1

Submit

Scan the QR code and submit tricky vocabulary words from your phone. Add and send as many words as you can!

2

Describe

Give spoken clues to your teammate before the timer runs out! No acting allowed!

3

Win

The team that correctly guesses the most words wins the game.

Strict Rules & Fouls

🗣️

NO Saying the Word: You cannot say the word, any part of the word, or translate it at any time.

🎭

NO Acting: You must describe the word using spoken clues only. No pantomiming or acting allowed!

👉

NO Pointing: You cannot point at the physical object in the room. This is an immediate FOUL!

🚫

NO Outside Help: You cannot join groups together or ask for help outside your pair. Doing so means you automatically lose a turn!

🔠

NO Spelling or Rhyming: You cannot say "It starts with the letter..." or "It sounds like...". You must describe the meaning!

🎙️

Clear Pronunciation: The guesser must pronounce the word clearly to make it count, otherwise the team loses a turn.

⏱️

Pass Penalty: You can pass if you're stuck, but passing a word will cost 2 seconds of your remaining time.

🎁

Time Bonus: Every correct guess immediately adds +1 second to your timer!

🏆

Tiered Scoring: Harder words give more points! B1+ = 1 pt, B2 = 2 pts, B2+ = 3 pts, C1 = 4 pts, C2 = 5 pts.
