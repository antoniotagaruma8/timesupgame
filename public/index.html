Time's Up!

Classroom Vocabulary Challenge

How to Play

1

Submit

Scan the QR code and submit tricky vocabulary words from your phone. Add and send as many words as you can!

2

Describe

Give clues and act out the words to your teammate before the timer runs out!

3

Win

The team that correctly guesses the most words wins the game.

Strict Rules & Fouls

🗣️

NO Saying the Word: You cannot say the word, any part of the word, or translate it.

👉

NO Pointing: You cannot point at the physical object in the room. This is an immediate FOUL!

⏱️

Pass Penalty: You can pass if you're stuck, but passing a word will cost 2 seconds of your remaining time.

🎁

Time Bonus: Every correct guess immediately adds +1 second to your timer!

🏆

Tiered Scoring: Harder words give more points! B1+ = 1 pt, B2 = 2 pts, C1 = 3 pts.
